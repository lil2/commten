Privacy Policy

===Commten Privacy Policy===

This policy describes how Commten uses and handles your information.

==Information used by Commten==

When you create an account with Commten, the following information is required:
- Username
- Password
- Name
- E-mail address
- Physical address
You do not have to use your real name on Commten.

==Why this Information is Collected==

This information is associated with your account, and is necessary for Commten to function. Physical address is needed to connect your account to your apartment management in the system. E-mail address is needed for verification purposes. The name you provide will be displayed in the application on your tickets.

==Data Deletion==

You can delete your account with Commten by clicking the "Delete Account" button in the Account Info page. When you delete your account, all account and ticket data is cleared.

==System Permissions==

Commten requires use of the device camera and photo gallery in order to allow attaching images to tickets.
